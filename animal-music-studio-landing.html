Built For Repeatable Creative Workflows

Source-first generation

The product starts with real audio instead of generic prompts, preserving recognizable timbre and giving creators more control.

Transparent selection

Clips are ranked by pitch clarity, tonal fit, and musical usefulness so users can understand why a sound was selected.

Creator-ready outputs

The workflow produces reusable audio assets, captions, metadata, and later short-form video packages for social channels.
